Welcome to our Mood Playlist Picker! You can generate a playlist by specifying the Valence and Energy you want.

Valence refers to positive and negative emotions. A high valence score will provide you with songs with positive emotion. A low valence score will provide songs with negative emotion.

Energy refers to energy level. A high energy score will provide you with energetic songs, while a low energy score will provide you with mellow songs.
